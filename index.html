Positive & Negative Feedback:

Feedback Network:

Summary:

Student feedback on Anna University's result-related processes indicates persistent frustrations. The university’s result portal frequently becomes sluggish or unresponsive during peak result periods, causing delays and anxiety. The revaluation system, despite being an opportunity for appeal, is criticized for its inconsistent outcomes and failure to refund fees even when grade changes occur. Additionally, students report high pressure regarding answer presentation and worry over stringent or opaque evaluation practices.

Conclusion:

Anna University remains a top choice for aspiring engineers and technologists, offering a blend of tradition, innovation, and excellence. Its legacy, combined with modern educational practices, ensures that students receive a holistic and future-ready education.
