Student Feedback

1. Classroom Quality & Comfort

Feedback: Most classrooms have decent seating, ventilation, and lighting, creating a good learning environment.

Impact: Comfortable classrooms help students stay focused and improve participation in lectures.

Improvement: Upgrade older furniture, improve air circulation in congested rooms, and maintain consistent lighting quality.

2. Laboratory Facilities

Feedback: Laboratories are well-equipped for academic needs, enabling practical learning.

Impact: Modern labs enhance hands-on skills, boost innovation, and support better research output.

Improvement: Ensure regular maintenance, replace outdated equipment, and introduce advanced tools for modern research.

3. Library Resources

Feedback: The library offers a rich collection of books, digital resources, and quiet study spaces.

Impact: Accessible resources improve academic performance and support independent learning.

Improvement: Expand e-book access, add more subject-specific materials, and increase seating capacity during exam periods.

4. Campus Cleanliness & Hygiene

Feedback: Common areas are generally clean, contributing to a healthy campus atmosphere.

Impact: Clean surroundings promote good health and create a positive impression for visitors.

Improvement: Increase the frequency of cleaning in washrooms, canteens, and high-traffic zones.

5. Hostel/Accommodation

Feedback: Hostel rooms provide basic living facilities with adequate safety.

Impact: Comfortable and safe accommodation helps students focus on studies without worrying about living conditions.

Improvement: Improve food quality, add healthier menu options, and upgrade room furnishings.

6. Canteen & Food Services

Feedback: Affordable meals are available, serving the daily needs of students.

Impact: Good quality food boosts student health and satisfaction, directly affecting academic energy levels.

Improvement: Enhance food hygiene, taste, and variety while keeping prices reasonable.

7. Sports & Recreation Facilities

Feedback: Students have access to indoor and outdoor games, as well as a gym.

Impact: Sports facilities encourage fitness, teamwork, and stress relief.

Improvement: Modernize sports equipment, maintain playgrounds, and expand gym capacity.

8. Wi-Fi & Internet Connectivity

Feedback: Campus Wi-Fi supports most academic requirements.

Impact: Strong internet connectivity enables online learning, research, and faster academic communication.

Improvement: Increase internet speed, improve coverage in remote areas, and reduce downtime.

9. Safety & Security

Feedback: CCTV coverage and security guards help maintain safety.

Impact: Good security ensures peace of mind for students and parents, encouraging a safe study environment.

Improvement: Increase security patrols, add lighting in darker areas, and improve emergency response systems.

10. Events & Cultural Activities

Feedback: The university hosts diverse cultural events, fostering inclusivity.

Impact: Cultural exposure improves confidence, creativity, and social connections among students.

Improvement: Organize more frequent activities and encourage participation from all departments.

11. Placement & Career Support

Feedback: Job fairs and internship programs connect students with employers.

Impact: Strong placement support increases employability and career readiness.

Improvement: Strengthen industry partnerships and offer more skill-development workshops.

12. Overall Campus Atmosphere

Feedback: The campus is friendly, culturally diverse, and academically engaging.

Impact: A positive atmosphere improves mental well-being, student retention, and community spirit.

Improvement: Continue promoting inclusivity, student comfort, and mental well-being initiatives.
