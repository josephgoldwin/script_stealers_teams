About

Anna University, established on 4 September 1978 and named after former Tamil Nadu Chief Minister Dr. C.N. Annadurai, is one of India’s premier technical universities. It was formed by integrating four historic institutions—the College of Engineering, Guindy (est. 1794), Alagappa College of Technology (1944), Madras Institute of Technology (1949), and the School of Architecture and Planning (1957). Headquartered at the Guindy campus in Chennai, with additional campuses in Chromepet, Taramani, and regional centers across Tamil Nadu, the university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, doctoral, and distance education programs.

Renowned for its academic excellence, research initiatives, and strong industry collaboration, Anna University consistently ranks among the top institutions in India and features in global rankings such as QS and THE. With modern infrastructure, advanced laboratories, vibrant student life, and a legacy of producing distinguished alumni including Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the university continues to uphold its mission of fostering innovation, technical competence, and ethical values for societal development.

Services

Anna University offers a wide range of services to support students, faculty, and researchers in their academic and professional growth:

  • Well-equipped libraries, digital learning platforms, and state-of-the-art laboratories.
  • Placement and career guidance services, internships, and job opportunities.
  • Hostel facilities, sports complexes, and healthcare services.
  • Research and innovation support through specialized centers and funding opportunities.
  • Online portals and administrative services for accessible processes.
